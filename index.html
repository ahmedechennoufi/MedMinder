Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medication?

Tap the plus button on the Today or Medications tab. Fill in the medication name, dosage, frequency, and set your reminder times. You can also customize the color and icon for easy identification.

How do I set up reminders?

When adding or editing a medication, you can set multiple reminder times. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for MedMinder to receive alerts.

How do I track a dose?

On the Today screen, tap the Take button next to any medication when you take your dose. You can also mark doses as skipped if needed.

How do I set up refill reminders?

When adding a medication, enable Refill Reminder and set your current pill count. The app will alert you when your supply reaches the threshold you specify.

Can I track multiple medications?

Yes, you can add unlimited medications to MedMinder. Each medication has its own schedule, reminders, and tracking history.

How do I view my medication history?

Go to the History tab to see your dose records by date. You can filter by status to see taken, missed, or skipped doses.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your medication data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal health information.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings and tap Export Data. You can share your medication list and dose history via email or save it for your records.

I am not receiving notifications. What should I do?

Please check that notifications are enabled for MedMinder in your device Settings. Go to Settings, then Notifications, find MedMinder, and ensure Allow Notifications is turned on.

How do I delete a medication?

Open the medication details, tap the menu icon in the top right corner, and select Delete. This will remove the medication and its dose history.

Troubleshooting

App is running slowly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device. Make sure you have the latest version of MedMinder installed.

Data appears to be missing

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you recently updated or reinstalled the app, previous data may not be recoverable. We recommend regularly exporting your data as a backup.
