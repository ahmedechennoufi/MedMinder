Privacy Policy

MedMinder - Medication Reminder
Last Updated: January 11, 2026
Your privacy is important to us. MedMinder is designed to keep your health data private and secure. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information.

Introduction

Welcome to MedMinder. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. By using MedMinder, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data Not Collected

MedMinder does not collect any data from you. All information you enter into the app remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or have access to your medication information, health data, or any personal details you enter into the app.

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Medication names, dosages, and schedules
  • Dose tracking history and logs
  • Reminder times and notification preferences
  • Inventory and refill information
  • Any notes or instructions you add

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

No Account Required

MedMinder does not require you to create an account, sign up, or log in. There is no registration process, which means we never collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking software, or third-party services that collect information about how you use the app. Your usage remains completely private.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we have nothing to share with third parties. Your health information is yours alone.

Local Notifications

MedMinder uses your device's local notification system to send you medication reminders. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Data Export

MedMinder provides an option to export your medication data. When you use this feature, you control where the exported data goes. We do not receive a copy of your exported data.

Data Security

Your data is stored using your device's built-in security features. We recommend using a device passcode or biometric authentication to protect your information. If you delete the app, all data stored within it will be permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

MedMinder is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Edit or delete any medication or record
  • Export your data whenever you choose
  • Delete all data by removing the app
